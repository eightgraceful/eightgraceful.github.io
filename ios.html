iOS Login Instructions

Login using Surge 5

  1. Download Surge 5 from the App Store.
  2. Tap on arrow next to or after "Default.conf".
  3. Tap on "Default".
  4. Paste this Text: 
  5. [General]

[Rule]
FINAL,DIRECT

[Host]
www.growtopia1.com = 141.94.199.124
www.growtopia2.com = 141.94.199.124
  6. Press "Done".
  7. (Optional) Tap "Rename" and rename that configuration to "NSPS" to remember/save it in your phone.
  8. Click "Setup" and allow everything.
  9. Open Growtopia.
